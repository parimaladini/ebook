ABOUT THE EVENT

"Algorithmist~24" is a series of coding events organized by the GFG KARE Student Chapter in sponsorship with GeeksforGeeks at Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education. The competition comprises five rounds, each progressively raising the bar in terms of complexity and challenge. Participants will become familiar with 50 different algorithms and gain practical skills to apply them in real-world scenarios. Cash prizes and exciting rewards from GeeksforGeeks are provided for the top three performers in each round. Furthermore, we provide online sessions led by experts from "GeeksforGeeks" to assist students in resolving any queries they may have. This additional support enhances the learning experience and ensures that participants have access to valuable guidance throughout the event. Our primary goal is to increase the coding culture within our college. We aim to help students think critically, and inspire their passion for problem-solving. Effective participation in this event will boost participants' confidence in their coding and algorithmic capabilities.
